Let me introduce myself

Being a UX/UI designer with journalism and customer service background, I always try to understand the cause, and not just to solve problems and deal with the consequences, whether I worked on a human interest story or a new software solution for call administration. Developing the skill of listening to understand and then solve the issues helped me a lot in my previous work, and it has been one of the crucial skills I transferred to my new career as a UX/UI designer. With this new expertise I gained during the UX and UI courses at CareerFoundry, I want to continue helping others and create new products and services that will improve users life quality.
